About Me

Hey there!!! I am Martha the girl behind the lens! I became a photographer because my grandmother and I shared the same vision. We loved capturing moments through portable cameras! We enjoyed the fact that we were able to print the images and would get so excited to pick them up once they were ready. I felt so cool! When my grandma passed away, I decided to buy a small camera and capture moments behind a real lens. I wanted to carry on with our passion. With my grandmother’s passing, I realized I never took a picture with her. This was the worst feeling ever! This is when I decided I wanted to give memories to everyone else. I love to capture family moments through any occasion. Moments like these are priceless and irreplaceable!

I started to capture moments of my own kids and few family members started to ask for portraits! This is when it became clear… I knew what I loved to do! I wanted to shoot weddings but I didn’t know where to start. So, I decided to reach out too few photographers in the valley to see if they would take me along. Honestly, this was the best decision I made and learned so many new things!! Thanks to those who gave me the opportunity to second shoot, I got the courage to step out of my comfort zone and finally capture magical wedding moments!
Why? Because omgshhh every bride is beautiful!

Every detail is important to me! Your wedding dress, ring, flowers, smiles, dances… YOU NAME IT! I will capture it ALL for you. Allow me to freeze this magical moment to reveal just how rich reality really is… I can’t wait to meet you and help capture your special day!
