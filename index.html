Hey it's Martha

I am Martha, the girl behind the camera! I am a mom of four angels and they are my favorite to work with! I am here to capture the best moment of your life not just an image! I can't wait to meet the entire family no matter what type of event we will be shooting!!! I love to capture a lifestyle photography because being yourself is better than a pose! I want to thank you for trusting me to capture this special moment!
